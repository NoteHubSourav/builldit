  How It Works

6 Steps.
Zero Friction.

From registration to payment — the entire workflow runs on Adhocsy, eliminating middlemen at every step.

1
Register & Verify

Workers create a verified profile using Aadhaar, build a digital work identity, and join the nearest cluster.

2
Post Shifts

Employers post shifts in under 2 minutes — location, pay, timing, and job type. Instantly visible to nearby workers.

3
Instant Match

Cluster-based algorithms match verified workers to nearby shifts in real time. No calls. No agencies.

4
OTP Gate Entry

Workers verify attendance via OTP at the gate. Digital, tamper-proof, and zero manual paperwork.

5
Complete & Get Paid

Shift wages transferred to the worker's digital wallet within 24 hours of verified completion.

6
Refer & Grow

Earn referral rewards by growing the cluster network. Multiple income streams for top Adhocsians.
